FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to give you access to my Google account?
You connect your Google Business Profile with one click. We only access your reviews — not your email or other data. You can revoke access anytime from your Google settings.
Do you post responses automatically or do I approve them?
By default, we review every AI-generated response before posting. Once you are confident in our quality — usually after the first 2 weeks — you can switch to fully automatic posting.
What platforms do you cover?
We focus exclusively on Google Business Profile — the platform that matters most for local search rankings and new customer decisions in India. We may add other platforms in future based on client demand.
How quickly do you respond to a new review?
New reviews are detected within minutes. We aim to post a response within 2 hours. Crisis reviews (1-star) are treated as urgent and handled within 60 minutes.
Can I see what responses are being posted?
Yes — your client portal shows every review and every response we have posted, along with timestamps and the original AI draft before editing. Full transparency.
