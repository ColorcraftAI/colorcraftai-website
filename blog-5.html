5 Things on Your Google Business Profile Hurting Your Ranking Right Now

4 min read · January 2025 · By ColorcraftAI

Most local businesses are losing customers every day to competitors with better-optimised Google Business Profiles. Here are the 5 most common mistakes — and each one takes less than 5 minutes to fix.

1

Your photos are old or missing

Google's algorithm favours profiles with recent, high-quality photos. If your last photo was uploaded 2 years ago — or you have fewer than 10 photos — you are losing ranking. Add at least 5 new photos today: exterior, interior, team, products/services, and customers (with permission).

Fix time: 10 minutes
2
2

You haven't answered Questions & Answers

The Q&A section of your Google Business Profile appears prominently in search results. Unanswered questions make you look unresponsive. Worse — anyone can answer them, including your competitors. Go through and answer every question, and proactively add your own Q&As covering common queries.

Fix time: 15 minutes
3

Your business category is wrong or incomplete

Your primary Google Business category is one of the most important ranking factors for local search. Many businesses use a generic category when a more specific one exists. A "Dental Clinic" ranks better than a generic "Health" category. Check your primary category and add up to 9 additional categories.

Fix time: 5 minutes
4

You're not responding to reviews

Google measures your response rate and response time. Businesses that respond to more than 75% of reviews consistently rank higher than those that don't. Every unanswered review is a missed signal to Google — and a missed opportunity to show future customers that you care.

Fix time: Ongoing — ColorcraftAI handles this
5

Your business description isn't optimised

The 750-character business description is prime real estate. Most businesses either leave it blank or write something generic. Include your main services, your location, what makes you different, and naturally include keywords your customers search for.

Fix time: 10 minutes
