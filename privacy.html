Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

ColorcraftAI is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Business information — name, city, contact email and phone
  • Google Business Profile data — your public Google reviews accessed via Google API with your authorisation
  • Usage data — how you interact with your dashboard and chatbot conversations
  • Payment information — processed securely. We do not store card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To monitor and respond to your Google reviews on your behalf
  • To generate AI-powered review responses using Claude (Anthropic)
  • To provide your reputation dashboard and weekly reports
  • To improve our AI chatbot responses over time (anonymised)

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google APIs — to access your Google reviews
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — to generate review responses and power our chatbot
  • Supabase — to store your data securely
  • Vercel — to host our platform

4. Data Security

Your data is stored on secure encrypted servers using HTTPS. Your data is never sold or shared outside service delivery.

5. Your Rights

  • Access — request a copy of your data
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion when you cancel
  • Portability — export your review history from your dashboard

6. Contact

Privacy questions: info@colorcraftai.in
