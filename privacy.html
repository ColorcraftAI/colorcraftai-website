Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · Effective immediately

ColorcraftAI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user or client of our Google reputation management service.

1. Data We Collect and Process

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Business profile data — including Google reviews and ratings from your Google Business Profile
  • Customer feedback and review content — text and metadata from reviews posted about your business
  • User account information — name, email address, business name, city, and contact details provided at sign-up
  • Usage and analytics data — how you interact with your client dashboard and which features you use
  • Payment information — billing details processed securely via Razorpay. We do not store your card details.

2. Google Business Profile Data

We access and process Google Business Profile data (including reviews and ratings) to provide our reputation management service. This data is accessed via Google APIs with your explicit authorisation. We only use this data to deliver the service you have signed up for — we do not sell or share it with any third party outside of service delivery.

By using ColorcraftAI, you authorise us to access your Google Business Profile reviews and ratings on your behalf to monitor, analyse, and respond to reviews as part of the service.

3. AI Processing

We use AI models to analyse reviews and generate suggested responses and insights. Review content may be processed by AI systems (including Anthropic's Claude) solely for the purpose of generating response drafts and reputation insights for your business.

AI-generated responses are always presented to you as suggestions. You retain full control over what is published on your Google Business Profile.

4. Payment Processing

Payments are processed securely via Razorpay. We do not store your card details or banking information on our servers. All payment transactions are handled directly by Razorpay in accordance with PCI-DSS standards.

For queries related to payment or billing, contact us at info@colorcraftai.in.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate ColorcraftAI:

  • Google APIs — for review data (Google Business Profile reviews and ratings)
  • Razorpay — for payments
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — to generate review responses and power our AI chatbot
  • Supabase — to store your review and client data securely
  • Vercel — hosting provider

Each provider has their own privacy policy. We share only the minimum data necessary to operate the service.

6. How We Use Your Information

  • To monitor and respond to your Google reviews on your behalf
  • To generate AI-powered review responses
  • To provide your reputation dashboard and usage insights
  • To improve our AI response quality over time (using anonymised data only)
  • To send billing invoices and service-related communications

7. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. We use industry-standard practices including HTTPS encryption and strict access controls. Your Google reviews data is never sold or shared with third parties outside of service delivery.

8. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at info@colorcraftai.in.

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data when you cancel
  • Portability — Export your review history at any time from your dashboard
  • Objection — Object to processing of your personal data in certain circumstances

We will respond to all data rights requests within 7 business days.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you cancel, we delete your personal data within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated usage data may be retained longer to improve our service.
