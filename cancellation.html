Cancellation and Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2025 - Effective immediately

ColorcraftAI is a subscription-based SaaS platform. No physical goods are sold or shipped. All services are delivered digitally.

1. Free Trial

All new clients receive the first month completely free. No credit card required. Cancel any time during the trial at zero cost.

2. Cancellation Policy

Cancel any time by emailing info@colorcraftai.in with subject "Cancel Subscription". Cancellations take effect at end of the current billing period. No pro-rated cancellations for partial months.

3. Refund Policy

  • Free trial: No charges. Cancel any time at zero cost.
  • First paid month: Full refund if requested within 7 days of first charge. No questions asked.
  • Subsequent months: Non-refundable once billing period has started, as services have been rendered.
  • Service disruption: If ColorcraftAI is down more than 48 consecutive hours in a billing month, we apply a pro-rated credit to your next cycle.

4. Refund Processing

Approved refunds processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method. Your bank may take an additional 3-5 days.

5. Disputed Transactions

Contact info@colorcraftai.in within 30 days of the transaction. We respond within 2 business days. Please resolve billing issues with us before filing a bank dispute.

6. Contact for Refunds

  • Email: info@colorcraftai.in
  • Response time: Within 2 business days
